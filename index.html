How to Play

⚔ Controls

  • ← → or A D — Move
  • Z or Space — Jump
  • X — Swing your sword
  • C — Throw a blade (costs 3◈)
  • E — Buy or drink a Cinder Draught

◈ Coins

  • Pick up coins scattered across the floor
  • Ground coins give 1◈, platform coins give 2◈
  • Kill enemies for bonus coins (1–3◈)
  • Spend wisely — throws cost coins too
  • Die and you lose half your coins

⚗ Cinder Draught

  • A healing tonic — available from Floor 3 onward
  • Find the glowing Shrine ⚗ on the floor and press E to buy one for 18◈
  • Press E anywhere to drink it — restores ~45 HP
  • Each use after the first heals a little less

🗝 Goal

  • Fight through enemies to find the Key
  • Some floors have a Mini-Boss — defeat it to unlock the door
  • Grab the key, then reach the Exit Door
  • Clear all five floors and take down the Ashen King

👑 Mini-Bosses & The Ashen King

  • Mini-Bosses are large, heavily armoured enemies — much tougher than regular enemies. They appear on Floor 3 (Iron Warden) and block the exit until defeated.
  • The Ashen King is the final boss on Floor 5. He has two phases — he gets faster and starts shooting when low on health. Beat him to win.
